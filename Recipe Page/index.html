Pasta Recipe

Pasta is a beloved and versatile food originating from Italy, made primarily from wheat flour and water, sometimes enriched with eggs. Its allure lies in the vast array of shapes and sizes it comes in, each designed to complement different sauces and ingredients. From long strands like spaghetti and fettuccine to short shapes like penne and fusilli, pasta is a culinary chameleon that can adapt to any dish.

Preparation Time

  • Total : Approximately 30 minutes
  • Preparation : 10 minutes
  • Cooking : 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoon butter or olive oil
  • Optional veggies: Cheese diced, Parmesan,Tomatoes vegetables,herbs

Instructions

1.

Cook the Pasta : Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

2.

Prepare the Sauce : While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.

3.

Add Tomatoes : Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs.Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.

4.

Combine Pasta and Sauce : Add the cooked and drained pasta to the skillet with the tomato sauce. Toss everything together, ensuring the pasta is evenly coated with the sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.

5.

Garnish and Serve : Remove the skillet from heat. Sprinkle the chopped fresh basil leaves over the pasta and toss again. If desired, top with grated Parmesan cheese before serving.
